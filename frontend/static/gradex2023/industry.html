The Best Place To Find Creative Talent

Welcome to GradEx - this is just one example of the many ways OCAD U supports and celebrates students as they begin their creative careers. OCAD U’s Graduate Exhibition is more than just an art and design exhibition – it’s a great place to scout for new creative talent or purchase work for your art collection.

Looking for a specific skillset or an art style or medium? OCAD U's RBC Centre for Emerging Artists & Designers will work to connect you with recent grads.

Give us a shout by emailing careerdevelopment@ocadu.ca or pop by our office at 115 McCaul St., Level 3, Thursday, May 4 or Friday, May 5 from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. during GradEx 108.
